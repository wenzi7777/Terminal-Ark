# Printing Level | 列印等階

Printing Level represents how well your account can handle molecular assembly work. It affects the quality ceiling of equipment you can obtain through printing-related systems.

列印等階代表你的帳號對分子裝配作業的掌握程度，會影響透過列印相關系統取得裝備時的品質上限。

---

## Level Cap | 等階上限

- Printing Level cannot exceed your current Main Level.
- If your Main Level rises first, Printing Level can continue growing until it catches up.
- When Printing Level is below Main Level, the Molecular Assembler will show a low-level warning.

- 列印等階最高不會超過目前的主等階。
- 如果主等階先提升，列印等階可以繼續成長，直到追上主等階。
- 當列印等階低於主等階時，分子裝配室會顯示等階偏低提示。

## How To Raise It | 如何提升

1. Print equipment at the Molecular Assembler. Each successful print can help build Printing EXP through the printing loop.
2. Go to the Lower Deck and work at the Molecular Assembler Center to gain Printing EXP over time.
3. Keep Main Level progressing as well, because it defines the maximum Printing Level you can reach.

1. 在分子裝配室列印裝備。每次成功列印都能透過列印循環累積列印經驗。
2. 前往下層甲板，到分子裝配中心內工作，隨時間獲得列印經驗。
3. 同時維持主等階成長，因為主等階會決定列印等階能達到的最高上限。

## Why It Matters | 為什麼重要

Higher Printing Level improves your access to better equipment results. If your Printing Level falls behind your Main Level, raising it is one of the clearest ways to improve future gear quality.

更高的列印等階能讓你取得更好的裝備製作結果。如果列印等階落後於主等階，優先提升列印等階會是改善後續裝備品質的明確方向之一。
